# About

I'm Geonwoo Kim — a data analyst working in the operational layer of one of
the world's largest EV battery manufacturers. My job sits between the people
running the business and the systems that record it: I make sure the numbers
flowing into financial reports, planning cycles, and cross-team dashboards
are right, and that the analyses built on top of them are pointed at the
right question.

## Trajectory

I came into this work through a combination of business operations and data
engineering. Over time, I noticed the most valuable thing I did wasn't the
SQL or the ETL pipeline itself — it was deciding which problems were worth
instrumenting in the first place, and translating between the people who
owned the business outcome and the systems that could measure it. That
product-shaped instinct is what I'd like the next role to lean into.

## What I'm optimizing for

Roles where technical fluency is a baseline, not the differentiator — where
the work involves owning a problem end-to-end: defining it, shaping the
analysis, building the case, and seeing it land as a decision. **Senior
Product Associate (Digital)**, **Technical Program Manager**, and **Senior
Business Analyst** tracks fit this shape, particularly at firms in finance,
fintech, and platform-heavy software.

## Toolkit

**Core** — SQL · Python (pandas, numpy) · ETL design · SAP (FI/CO modules)

**Adjacent** — Excel modeling · Power BI / Tableau · Git · A/B framing & basic statistics

**Languages** — Korean (native) · English (professional)
